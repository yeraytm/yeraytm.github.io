Triune VR

My Role

During the development of Triune VR I worked on the VR interaction and functionality, integrating the necessary systems to process users' input and how they interact with the environment through the VR headset.

About the Project

Triune VR is a university project where we developed a demo taking into account contents that we learned during the theoretical course about the fundamentals of VR & AR experiences and the immersion that they bring to the digital interactive media.

It was a simple demo made in a month to showcase the Unity implementation of VR packages from a group of 4 students.
